I won a contest and had my name published in a magazine ad with some of my code (and received a free copy of the magazine). I saw the magazine in the store and decided to check it out. The magazine was Contract Professional and it had an article "Projects from Hell." Since I was never involved in any disaster projects I thought it may be wise to be prepared incase I ever ended up in a situation like that. Maybe spot signs early? Who knows?



There was an ad that had some code which displayed "IT doesn't get any better than this!" and you could figure it out because it was a recursive function and all the text was there. Well when I went to the web site to enter the answer they challenged me to come up with a better code puzzle. Since I'm a coder that is precisely what I did!



I used a formula to encode the letters. And just to make sure no one could figure it out I included a random number (which isn't really random) so the only way to get the answer would be to run it on the computer. I chose a random number seed that would include a quote in the encrypted text for a little extra something. It says "Bet you needed more than a pencil and paper to get this one!" I won.
