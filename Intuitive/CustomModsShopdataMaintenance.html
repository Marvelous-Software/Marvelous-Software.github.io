A simple, yet significant change was needed for the shop data maintenance. Since the U.S. Army is a customer, their regulations require periodic inspections. During one such inspection they became concerned when an employee enters their hours for a work order. Since the computers in the shop are shared, different employees can use the same ERP login. This gave the possibility of an employee entering hours for someone else. We never had that problem, however the army wished to eliminate the possibility of that happening to ensurre accountability. So a password field was added to the employee card and when the user selects an employee to log hours for (always themselves) a password dialog appears and will not let the user procede until they enter their password.
