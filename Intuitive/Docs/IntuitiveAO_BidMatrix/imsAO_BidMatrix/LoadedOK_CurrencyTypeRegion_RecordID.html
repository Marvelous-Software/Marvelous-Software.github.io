LoadedOK_CurrencyTypeRegion_RecordID

Member resides in the imsAO_BidMatrix Class

Summary


Update the logical field lCurrencyTypeRegion_RecordID based on the currencyType and region.

Remarks


When the currencytype and region are not found in the joined ao CurrencyTypeRegion the logical field is updated to guid.empty. This could happen because the currencyType/region combination does not exist or is inactive.
