ReverseCommissions

Member resides in the imsAO_Invoice Class

Summary


Called from Save_Ancillary during the Reverse Invoice function.

Remarks


The routine first does a join between the invoice lines and the commission transactions. This is done to minimize database hits and needless attempts to instantiate commission objects. The data table returned from the join is then used to guide the reversal process. As the record ids are known, the routine takes advantage of that fact and instantiates a minimum of commission objects.
