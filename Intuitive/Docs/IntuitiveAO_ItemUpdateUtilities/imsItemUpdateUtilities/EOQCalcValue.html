EOQCalcValue

Member resides in the imsItemUpdateUtilities Class

Summary


Calculate the EOQ here with these steps Top Line of Formula Step 1 - Multiply avg monthly usage by 24 ("AvgMonthlyUsage" in itemTable) Step 2 - Multiply usage by order cost Bottom Line of Formula Step 3 - Multiply Accounting value of the item by carrying cost ("IMA_AcctValAmt" in itemTable) Must make sure that acctg value is NOT zero Step 4 - Divide step 2 results by step 3 results Step 5 - Take square root of step 4 results and write EOQ value to itemTable

Remarks


24 is used as a constant because the APICS formula is annual demand * 2. So I have multiplied monthly usage times 24 to get 2 years of demand.
