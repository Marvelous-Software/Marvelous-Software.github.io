CalculateLeadTime

Member resides in the imsAO_WorkOrder Class

Summary


Calculate the number of lead time days for the item. When the master control for Routing Hours is enabled and the item's Routing Hours Exception flag is true, the lead time is calculated using master controls and the items OverlapHrs, OutplantVarTime, StdSetupHrs, and ItemsPerSetup.

Returns


Integer: Lead Time always rounds up.
