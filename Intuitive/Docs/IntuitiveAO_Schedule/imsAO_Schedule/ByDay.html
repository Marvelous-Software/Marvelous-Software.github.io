ByDay

Member resides in the imsAO_Schedule Class

Summary


Create a schedule for the case when the recurrence pattern chosen is by day.

Remarks


The data used here is recurrence pattern 'Occurs Every X Days' from Schedule Start. Basically, the routine begins with the start date and then loops adding X days until the stop date is reached. For each scheduled date, the times are then determined. Optimization determines a 'window' within the schedule that is meaningful for determining if the time is due and limits determing the times to within the days in that window. If we are talking a schedule that pops every minute for several years this ends up saving many thousands of useless calculations.
